Julius Randle

Julius Randle is the Knicks star player. Averaging 24 points, 10 rebounds and 5 assist last season. Earning the Most Improved Player Award and his first All-Star game appearance.

Kemba Walker

Kemba is the newest member of the knicks and is one of the best point gaurds they have had in years. He has 4 All-Star appearances in his career.

RJ Barrett

RJ is the Knicks promising future star. He was drafted 3rd Overall in 2019 and had a great sophomore season where he helped lead the knicks to the 4th seed in the eastern conference.

Saquon Barkley

Saquon exploded onto the NFl scene winning the Offensive Rookie of the Year award when he was drafted second overall in 2018. However, he has recently been struggling with injuries but is still the Giants most skilled player.

Daniel Jones

Daniel Jones was controversially drafted 6th overall by the Giants in 2019. He has since proven that he deserved to be drafted that high, however, he still has a lot of work to do before being a franchise QB.

Sterling Shepard

Sterling Shepard was drafted in the second round of 2016 and was a great surprise on the Giants Offense. He has great hands and is a reliable reciever for any quarter back.

Matt Barzal

Matt Barzal is the Islanders most explosive player. He is an exceptional skater and playmaker. He won the NHL Rookie of the Year in 2018.

Adam Pelech

Adam Pelech may be the most important player on the Islanders. He is one of the best shut down defenseman in the league and leads the Islanders elite defensive unit.

Ilya Sorokin

Ilya Sorokin has been an Islanders prospect for years but was finally able to transfer from KHL last year. He did not dissappoint and was great in goal during their playoff series against the penguins last year.
